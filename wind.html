Wind Band

Join the Wind Band

Wednesdays, 4:30 - 6:00pm | CKB 116

Conducted by Mr. Nick Santoro, the wind band provides an opportunity for former band nerds to exercise their craft and meet other wind band musicians on campus. The wind band consists of all wind and brass instruments and includes upright bassists and percussionists. Their set lists include of musical classics, marches, ballads and more. They practice weekly at the Central King Building from 4:30pm to 6:00pm every Wednesday, culminating in a final performance at the end of each semester.
