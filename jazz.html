Jazz Band

Ya like Jazz?

Tuesdays, 7:00 - 9:00pm | CKB 116

Lead by Mr. Dave Remelis, the jazz band plays through funk, bepop, modern, blues, and more. The band consists of trumpets, saxaphones, violinists, hand drum percussionists, vibraphonists, bassists, guitarists, pianists, singers and flutists. However, musicians of all types are welcome. At the end of each semester, they perform a concert. They practice every Tuesday from 7:00 - 9:00pm at the Central King Building 116. For those who are interested, feel free to join their practices at anytime.
