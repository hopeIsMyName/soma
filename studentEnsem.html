Pep Band
Mondays
4:00pm-5:00
CC B49

The Pep Band is a student run organization lead by Andrew Argot and Hope De Jesus.

They perform for the NJIT women's and men's basketball team home games.

Practices are weekly on Mondays at 4:00-5:00pm in the Campus Center basement.

They accept members at anytime throughout the semester.

(And yes, we know their uniforms do look a lot like Waldo from the "Where is Waldo" children's book series.)
