Join the String Band

Tuesdays and Thursdays | CKB 116

Mr. Christine Sweet leads the string ensemble where students play pieces from a variety of time periods, including pieces composed by students in the ensemble themselves. Their ensemble consists of violins, violas, cellos, upright basses, pianists. In previous performances, they've collaborated with the Rutgers Choir.
