Musical Ensembles

The following three ensembles perform a final concert at the end of each semester.
Long term dedication and attendance can qualify for college credit.

Wind Band

Wednesdays | 4:00pm-6:00pm

Conducted by Mr. Nick Santoro, the wind band provides an opportunity for former band nerds to exercise their craft and meet other wind band musicians on campus.

String Ensemble

Tuesdays and Thursdays

Mr. Christine Sweet leads the string ensemble where students play pieces from a variety of time periods, including pieces composed by students in the ensemble themselves.

Jazz Band

Tuesdays | 7:00pm-9:00pm

Lead by Mr. Dave Remelis, the jazz band plays through funk, bepop, modern, blues, and more. All instruments and musicians of any skill set are welcome.

SOMA Events

When we're not studying and not practicing, SOMA members come together to run social musical events.
From our open mics to our game nights, SOMA events allow students to meet other musicians. All are always welcome.

Campfire Song Session

Last semester, we held a night of singing and s'mores. SOMA members played their guitars and djembes along to classic campfire songs.

Open Mic Nights

At least once a month, SOMA hosts open mic night at the NJIT pub. Students of all talents are welcome to perform.

Picnic on the Parking Deck

To celebrate the new school year, the e-board hosted a potluck parking deck picnic on the top floor of the Laurel parking deck.

Brooklyn Trip

Student run band Elementals traveled to a barcade in Brooklyn to listen and perform with a video game cover band.
