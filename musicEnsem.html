Music Ensembles

String Quartet
Tuesdays
7:00 - 9:00pm
CKB 116

Lead by Mr. Dave Remelis, the jazz band plays through funk, bepop, modern, blues, and more.

The band consists of trumpets, saxaphones, violinists, hand drum percussionists, vibraphonists, bassists, guitarists, pianists, singers and flutists.

However, musicians of all types are welcome.

At the end of each semester, they perform a concert.

Practice are held every Tuesday from 7:00 - 9:00pm at the Central King Building 116.

For those who are interested, feel free to join their practices at anytime.

Elementals
Wednesdays
4:30 - 6:00pm
CKB 116

Conducted by Mr. Nick Santoro, the wind band provides an opportunity for former band nerds to exercise their craft and meet other wind band musicians on campus.

The wind band consists of all wind and brass instruments and includes upright bassists and percussionists.

Their set lists include of musical classics, marches, ballads and more.

They practice weekly at the Central King Building from 4:30pm to 6:00pm every Wednesday, culminating in a final performance at the end of each semester (fall and spring).
